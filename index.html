About Us

Pragya Fashions is a business firm that has been offering services in the textile industry from the past six years. The company has managed to successfully provide direct employment opportunity to over 70 employees through textile manufacturing. So far the company was involved in the manufacturing of a variety of uniforms ranging from industrial wear, school uniforms to hospital linen.

We at Pragya Fashions, aim at providing quality clothing to domestic customers and markets with the help of a huge teams of well-versed people. We have well equipped infrastructure with skilled and experienced supervisors to curate our products to the best for our customers. We believe “QUALITY IS A HABIT “and ensure that it reflects on our company’s merchandise.
